Pooooong

Pooooong is a modern variant of a timeless classic with a twist. To survive, keep all the balls in play. Maximize your length. Kick as many balls as possible.

Relax in chill mode, get a hang of the game in normal mode or test your skills in hard mode. Pooooong features a recognizable, minimalistic design and short - can we say brutally short? - gameplay. Your hiscore is the real enemy.

Pooooong is currently available on the Google Play Store for Android 9 and higher.

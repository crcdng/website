ZAHLENSENDER

"eight"... "nine"... "four"... "two"... "two"... "eight"...

Remember numbers stations, the mysterious cold war radio stations broadcasting an endless stream of seemingly random numbers? With ZAHLENSENDER you get your very own numbers station. Listen to an endless stream of numbers for relaxation, to tingle your brain, or just for fun.

ZAHLENSENDER comes in three versions. Free with 1 voice. Premium has 3 voices. Ultra comes with 7 voices and with numbers visualization. Can you figure out the secret behind the numbers? Ultras can submit a solution and - if correct - enter their hall of fame below.

ZAHLENSENDER is COMING SOON on the Google Play Store for Android 10 and higher.
